ABOUT US

YouClubVideo was started as an idea to bring together a wide variety of clubbing experiences and people from all the countries of the world that have in common the same feelings, sounds and sences of club music.

Taking a next step from just weekend clubbing, YouClubVideo gives the opportunity to have that experience in any other day of the week at any time, furthermore giving you access to all of the clubbing ideas shared around the world, and also the people associated with that, being able to communicate and share your experiences through video, sounds and pictures.

It started in November 2008 bringing together three friends from a small city in Romania, that from there on began a step by step road to bring this idea into reality and make the best of what they believed to be the potential of a large community of people willing to share their personal experiences into what is to become the soul place to expand this community and also maintain it along the way so that nothing gets lost on the way, further pursuing the idea of All Time Clubbing.

YouClubVideo offers:

  • - Usage free of charge
  • - Unlimited storage space for uploads
  • - Integration of videos is easy and fast
  • - The video player can be implemented into your own website
  • - Additional and modern promotion channel ( WebTv )
  • - Support for HighDefinition videos
