ADE – Amsterdam Dance Event

Amsterdam Dance Event – ADE – is considered to be world`s biggest club music festival. Amsterdam Dance Event means hundreds of DJ’s and acts performing in Amsterdam’s finest venues, cafés, record stores, lounges and art galleries.

ADE – Amsterdam Dance Event Magazine

Such a big event needed similar promotion. Amsterdam Dance Event has launched a Magazine for those passionate about club music, about spending time in quality locations. The ADE Magazine is a full guide for the participants, containing full festival program details, interviews with Chris Liebing, Joris Voorn, Groove Armada, Kyle Hall and Karotte.

Amsterdam Dance Event 2009

Amsterdam Dance Event 2009 – the event is scheduled for 21 / 22 / 23 / 24 October 2009.

ADE Amsterdam Dance Event- Minus Night at The Powerzone – 2009

ADE Amsterdam Dance Event- Showcase at Odeon – 2009

ADE – Presents Innervisions at Trouw – 2009
